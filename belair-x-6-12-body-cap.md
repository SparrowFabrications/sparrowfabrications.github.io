## Belair X 6-12 Body Cap
A replacement body cap for Belair X 6-12 cameras. Camera shown is not included.

Designed by me from scratch to fit the Belair X 6-12 camera.

### Directions for use
This is a push and twist fit body cap, simply align the indicators, press gently into place and twist clockwise. Reverse this to remove.

### Supported Cameras
Tested with:
- Belair X 6-12 City Slicker (black plastic)

Should be compatible with:
- Belair X 6-12 Globe-trotter (snake-style patterned leather and metal)
- Belair X 6-12 Jet Setter (brown leather and metal)
- Belair X 6-12 Trailblazer (dark brown leather and metal)
